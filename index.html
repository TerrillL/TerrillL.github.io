Who are they?

Blizzard Entertainment is an American video game company that was founded in 1991 and is based in Irvine, CA. The company is known for creating well designed and highly enjoyable video games and have maintained an excellent reputation for quality since it's founding.

Blizzard Entertainment is the company behind some of the most iconic video games to this day:

  • World of Warcraft
  • StarCraft
  • Diablo
  • Overwatch
