The Warcraft series is a real time strategy series where the first title was originally released on November 23, 1994. The games original theme came from taking characters similar to the vikings from their previous game "The Lost Vikings" and combining with other creatures and having them meet up and fight each other. Warcraft: Orcs & Humans ranked 19th in the top 20 CDs accross all categories in November 1995 in the Entertainment Weekly. This title was companies greatest success during its time and made the company's finances secure for the first time.

World of Warcraft is a massively multiplayer online role-playing game that was originally released in November 2004 and takes place 4 years after the the ending of Warcraft III: The Frozen Throne. World of Warcraft won several rewards from critics and the media and was described as the best in the role playing and MMORPG genre. Blizzard Entertainment has released 6 expansions for World of Warcraft to expand upon it's story and has a 7th on the horizon.

  • Hearthstone: Heroes of Warcraft

Hearthstone is a free to play online collectible card game that was released in March 2014. Hearthstone was praised for it's simplicity, gameplay pace, and attention to detail along with being free to play. The game had recieved numerous "Mobile Game of the Year" awards in 2014.
