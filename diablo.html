The Diablo series is a role playing hack and slash dungeon crawler game that was released in 1996. It was praised for it's addictive gameplay, immense replayability, dark atmosphere, superior graphics, and great variety. The original Diablo had won "Game of the Year" in 1996 and in 2005 was chosen as one of "The Greatest Games of All Time" from Gamestop. By August 2001, DIablo had sold 2.5 million copies worldwide.

Diablo III was released in May 2012. It had set the record for fastest selling PC game with over 3.5 million sales within the first 24 hours of it's relase, sold a total of 6.3 million copies within it's first week, and by the end of 2012 had sold over 12 million copies. Diablo III still remains the fastest selling PC game to date.
