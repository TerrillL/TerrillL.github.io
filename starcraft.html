The StarCraft series is a military science fiction real time strategy game where the first title was released in March 1998. The original StarCraft was extremely popular after it's release and became the best selling PC game in 1998 winning numerous awards along the way. StarCraft was described by Gamespot as "The defining game of it's genre. It is the standard by which all real time strategy games are judged.". Blizzard had released a remastered version in August 2017.

StarCraft had a huge presence and impact on Esports because of the attention it had received following it's release in South Korea where it became the National Esports.
